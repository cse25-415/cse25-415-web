Our Cattle Breeds

Detailed information about each breed to help you make the right choice

Choosing the right breed is one of the most important decisions a farmer makes. At KraalPro, we offer a variety of breeds suited for beef production, dairy, and local African conditions.

Beef Cattle

Brahman

The Brahman is one of the most popular beef breeds in Southern Africa. It is well known for its ability to handle hot and humid climates, making it perfect for Botswana's weather conditions.

  • Excellent heat tolerance
  • Strong resistance to ticks and parasites
  • Good growth rate and meat quality
  • Easy to manage and calm temperament

Best for: Beef farming in hot, dry areas

Indigenous / African

Nguni

The Nguni is a traditional African breed that has been raised in Southern Africa for centuries. It is perfectly adapted to local conditions and is highly valued for its hardiness and low-maintenance nature.

  • Naturally adapted to African environment
  • Resistant to local diseases and insects
  • Requires less water and feed than exotic breeds
  • Colourful coat patterns, culturally significant

Best for: Small-scale farmers and traditional farming

Buy Nguni
Dual Purpose

Hereford

The Hereford is a well-known European breed with a white face and reddish-brown body. It is one of the most widely raised beef breeds in the world due to its efficient weight gain and docile nature.

  • Excellent feed efficiency
  • Fast weight gain
  • Calm and easy to handle
  • Good milk production for calves

Best for: Commercial beef production

Buy Hereford
Beef Cattle

Bonsmara

The Bonsmara was developed in South Africa and is one of the best-performing beef breeds in African conditions. It is a cross between Hereford, Shorthorn, and Afrikaner cattle.

  • Bred specifically for African conditions
  • High fertility rate in cows
  • Great carcass quality
  • Good mothering ability

Best for: Commercial ranchers seeking high productivity

Buy Bonsmara
Dairy Cattle

Holstein Friesian

The Holstein Friesian is the world's highest milk-producing dairy breed. Easily recognised by its black and white patches, it is the go-to choice for dairy farmers looking for maximum milk output.

  • World's top milk producer
  • Produces up to 30 litres per day
  • Good adaptability to farm management
  • Well-suited for commercial dairy operations

Best for: Dairy farming and milk production

Buy Holstein
