About KraalPro

Our story, our mission, and the people behind the brand

Who We Are

KraalPro is a Botswana-based cattle sales platform founded in 2018 by a group of passionate farmers and agriculture professionals. Our goal is simple: to connect buyers and sellers of quality cattle in an easy, transparent, and trustworthy way.

We understand the challenges of finding reliable cattle in Botswana. That is why KraalPro was built — to bring farmers together, remove the guesswork, and make every transaction safe and fair for both sides.

Whether you are a first-time farmer buying your very first heifer, or an experienced rancher expanding a commercial herd, KraalPro is here to help every step of the way.
