Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy cattle from KraalPro?

Browse the listings on our Shop page. When you find what you are looking for, click "Enquire Now" or fill in the Contact form above. Our sales team will follow up and guide you through the process.

Are the cattle vet certified?

Yes. All cattle listed on KraalPro are inspected by a registered veterinarian. Vaccination and health records are provided with every purchase.

Do you deliver cattle to my farm?

We assist with transport arrangements. Delivery cost depends on your location. Contact us for a delivery quote.

Can I sell my cattle through KraalPro?

Yes! Contact us to register as a seller. We will inspect your cattle and help list them on the platform to reach our network of buyers.
